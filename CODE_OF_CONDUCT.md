# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in the Playbook community a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions.
- Assume positive intent and provide actionable feedback.
- Focus on ideas, not individuals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Disruptive or intentionally hostile behavior.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, open a private security-style report through repository maintainers with relevant context.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and issues that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
