# Sevenfold Plus Ontology

## Purpose
Sevenfold Plus is a reusable symbolic ontology for worldbuilding, narrative systems, gameplay systems, and engine architecture.

It normalizes symbolic domains into a layered model that separates canon truth, esoteric references, and internal dev shorthand.

## Canonical Topology

- **0 = substrate/source** (pre-form)
- **1–7 = differentiated archetypes**
- **8 = synthesis/reintegration**
- **9 = persistence/legacy**

> **Rule:** 0, 8, and 9 are meta-states around the seven, not extra entries beside them.

---

## Layer Separation Contract

### Canon layer (player-facing)
Contains names, cosmology, symbols, factions, realms, quests, and consequences visible in the shipped world.

### Esoteric/reference layer (author-facing)
Contains comparative symbolic studies, historical correspondences, and cross-tradition mappings used only for internal design reasoning.

### Dev mnemonic layer (team-facing)
Contains shorthand labels, engineering nicknames, and implementation mnemonics.

> **Rule:** Dev mnemonic material must not appear in player-facing text, VO, UI copy, codex entries, or artifact flavor text.

---

## Meta-States

### 0 — The Quiet (Source/Void Before Form)
- **Core meaning:** undivided potential, silence, pre-identity.
- **Healthy expression:** stillness, receptivity, possibility.
- **Shadow expression:** nihilism, dissociation, refusal to incarnate.
- **World role:** abyss, fog, erased memory, substrate beneath all kingdoms.
- **System role:** null state, latent tags, world seed, pre-instantiated content space.

### 8 — The Octave (Mercy/Reintegration)
- **Core meaning:** return of the pattern at a higher order.
- **Healthy expression:** synthesis, forgiveness, resurrection, mature harmony.
- **Shadow expression:** false unity, purity tyranny, assimilation, sterile perfection.
- **World role:** reconciled realms, redeemed symbols, hidden true ending.
- **System role:** fusion rules, hybrid archetypes, purification transforms, postgame synthesis.

### 9 — The Fruit (Legacy/Living Kingdom)
- **Core meaning:** truth embodied in history, inheritance, stewardship, consequence.
- **Healthy expression:** maturity, culture, lineage, transmission, harvest.
- **Shadow expression:** calcification, empire, bureaucracy, dogma, canon as prison.
- **World role:** restored city, archive, descendants, history after the hero.
- **System role:** persistence layer, consequence graph, world memory, save continuity, lineage systems.

## Differentiated Seven (1–7)

> Shadows are interpreted as broken responses to separation from source.

### 1 — Crown of Self / Pride
- **Public name:** The Crown
- **Shadow name:** Pride
- **Redeemed form:** Vocation
- **Core meaning:** identity, aim, initiating will.
- **Core wound:** unworthy selfhood.
- **False promise:** invulnerability through superiority.
- **Healthy expression:** responsible leadership, clear direction.
- **Shadow expression:** domination, isolation, contempt.
- **Visual language:** vertical forms, crowns, sun-disks, single spire.
- **Audio language:** solo brass, high clear tones, declarative motifs.
- **Symbolic objects:** scepter, seal ring, oath blade.
- **Realm expression:** high citadel biome.
- **Enemy logic:** tries to become its own source.
- **Relic logic:** buffs initiative, cost if used selfishly.
- **Quest logic:** prove authority through service.
- **Narrative role:** caller/sovereign archetype.
- **Gameplay role:** opener, stance setter, frontline command.
- **Engine role:** authority and ownership tags.
- **Crossover rules:** harmonizes with 2, distorts 6.
- **Taboo/failure risk:** ruler cult; protagonist exceptionalism lock.

### 2 — Ledger of Need / Greed
- **Public name:** The Ledger
- **Shadow name:** Greed
- **Redeemed form:** Stewardship
- **Core meaning:** resource, exchange, protection.
- **Core wound:** scarcity panic.
- **False promise:** possession will end absence.
- **Healthy expression:** wise allocation, mutual provision.
- **Shadow expression:** hoarding, extraction, commodification.
- **Visual language:** scales, vaults, geometric tessellation.
- **Audio language:** clipped pulses, counting rhythms, metallic ticks.
- **Symbolic objects:** keyring, weight stones, contracts.
- **Realm expression:** salt-basin markets and vault-cities.
- **Enemy logic:** tries to possess enough to fill absence.
- **Relic logic:** converts drops to reserves with corruption threshold.
- **Quest logic:** redistribute cursed abundance.
- **Narrative role:** quartermaster/merchant prince.
- **Gameplay role:** economy, crafting, loadout pressure.
- **Engine role:** inventory, currencies, sink/source balancing.
- **Crossover rules:** harmonizes with 4, feeds 7 in shadow.
- **Taboo/failure risk:** pay-to-win feel; extraction fantasy dominance.

## Generation Guarantees
For each number, generation rules must emit at least:
- one realm or biome concept
- one boss or faction archetype
- one relic line
- one questline pattern
- one system/engine hook

Additional guardrails:
- **8** must generate reconciled or hybridized outputs, not duplicate standalone realms.
- **9** must generate persistence, consequence, legacy, lineage, or civilization outputs (never generic final-boss defaults).
